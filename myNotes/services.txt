Why do people add a Services layer?
The Service layer is where you put all the real business logic.
Think of it like this:

- Controller = "The waiter" :

Job: 
Take the order from the customer (the HTTP request).
Extract what is needed from req.body or req.params .
Call the kitchen (service).
Return the food nicely to the customer (send JSON response).
Handle errors and HTTP status codes.

- Service = "The chef / kitchen" :

Job:
Actually cook the food (do the real work).
Contains all business rules and logic.
Talks to the database (Prisma).
Can be reused by multiple controllers.
Much easier to test.
