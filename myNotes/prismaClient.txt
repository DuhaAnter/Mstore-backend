(set null on delete when deleting a category)

-- This is the most realistic behavior for an e-commerce store:

   1- When a Category is deleted, the relationship link inside CatSubCategory is deleted automatically (onDelete: Cascade).

   2- The Product stays alive, but its category fields are set to null so it becomes "Uncategorized" (onDelete: SetNull).

To make this work, the relation scalar fields in your Product model must be optional (String?).

-- if a category (like "Men") is deleted, the association pairing ("Men-Shoes") has no reason to exist anymore. this cleans up that pairing table automatically while preserving your actual Product records by safely setting their category references to null.
